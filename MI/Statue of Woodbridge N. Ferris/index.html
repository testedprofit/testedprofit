WOODBRIDGE
N. FERRIS.

Governor. Educator. Founder. This bronze monument at the heart of Big Rapids commemorates the man who believed education should be accessible to everyone.

Educational Visionary

Woodbridge Nathan Ferris served as the 28th Governor of Michigan, but his primary passion was the Ferris Industrial School. The statue captures his likeness as a man of action, facing the campus he built from the ground up.

Historical Pillar

Positioned at 1010 Campus Drive, the monument is more than a statue; it's a symbolic anchor for the $PNET Educational Asset Cluster, representing the preservation of mid-Michigan's intellectual history.
