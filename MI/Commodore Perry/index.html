The Battle

Commemorating the decisive 1813 victory on Lake Erie. Perry's fleet defeated the British, ensuring American control of the vital waterways.

The Park

Situated in Hood Park, a serene green space offering commanding views of the Maumee River, connecting history with nature conservation.

The Legacy

"Don't Give Up The Ship." The statue embodies the resilience and tactical brilliance that defined the early American navy.
