The Saint

Patron Saint of Ireland, known for bringing Christianity to the Emerald Isle. The statue represents faith, endurance, and cultural identity.

The Region

The "Irish Hills" of Michigan were named for their resemblance to the rolling green landscape of Ireland, attracting Irish immigrants in the 19th century.

The Site

Located in Onsted, often associated with the historic St. Joseph Shrine, a cornerstone of the local community since 1854.
