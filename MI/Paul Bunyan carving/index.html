The Folk Hero

Paul Bunyan, the giant lumberjack of American folklore. His legend represents the scale and might of the logging industry that cleared Michigan's forests in the 1800s.

The Carving

Unlike polished bronze statues, this monument is carved from wood—fitting for a lumberjack—capturing the raw, homespun aesthetic of roadside Americana.

The Setting

Located on S Antler St in Gladwin, a town deeply rooted in timber history. It stands as a quirky, beloved waymarker for locals and travelers alike.
