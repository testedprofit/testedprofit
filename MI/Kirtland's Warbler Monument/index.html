Mio, Michigan • Habitat Secure

PHOENIX OF
THE PINES.

A monument to the Kirtland's Warbler—the "Jack Pine Warbler"—and the conservationists who brought it back from the brink of extinction through fire and forestry.

Species Data Map Site
Status
Recovered

Jack Pine Specialist

The Warbler nests only on the ground under young Jack Pine trees. This monument celebrates the unique habitat management required to save them.

Born of Fire

Historically reliant on wildfires to regenerate pines, conservationists now mimic these conditions to provide nesting grounds.

Mio's Legacy

Located in the Huron-Manistee National Forest, Mio is the symbolic capital of the effort that brought the population from 167 males to over 2,300.
