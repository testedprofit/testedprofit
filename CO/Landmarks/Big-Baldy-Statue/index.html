Landmark Profile

Big Baldy
Statue

A majestic 2,800-pound bronze and copper sculpture of a bald eagle, standing as a symbolic guardian over the Dillon Reservoir and Tenmile Range.

Get Directions
Open 24/7
Summit County, CO

Dillon Reservoir Landmark

2,800 LBS
Bronze & Copper

Scenic Backdrop

Perfectly positioned to capture stunning views of the Tenmile Range and the reservoir. It is widely considered one of the best spots for sunrise and sunset photography in Dillon.

Park Amenities

Located within a "pocket park" on Tenderfoot St. Features include a convenient parking lot, waste disposal, and immediate access to the Dillon Lakeshore shoreline.

Accessibility

Designed for everyone. Features wheelchair-accessible parking and connects directly to the regional paved recreation path (Rec Path) for easy transit.
