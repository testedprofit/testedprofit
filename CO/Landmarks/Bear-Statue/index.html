The Genesis of a Landmark

Located at 12600 W Colfax Ave, Lakewood, CO, the scent of fresh wood and pine needles once defined this corner of the foothills. Barnaby, a magnificent wooden bear carved from a colossal ponderosa pine, stands as a local icon. His features are so lifelike that hikers often pause, half-expecting his eyes to twinkle in the Colorado sun.

Years ago, the founders of TestedProfit, Sarah and Tom, discovered this location during one of their "discovery hikes." They saw untapped potential in this lightning-struck tree, much like the untapped potential they find in every business they partner with.

They commissioned Elias, a master woodcarver, to breathe life into the timber. Each cut of his chisel mirrored the TestedProfit methodology: precise, purposeful, and designed to reveal the inherent strength buried beneath the surface.
