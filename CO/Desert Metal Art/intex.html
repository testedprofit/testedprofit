Desert Metal Art

A specialized retailer in Grand Junction offering a diverse collection of metal sculptures for home and landscape design.

Desert Metal Art is a locally owned business located in Grand Junction, Colorado, dedicated to the sale of metal sculptures and decorative yard art. The establishment focuses on providing distinctive, durable pieces suitable for both indoor and outdoor environments, ranging from small tabletop accents to monumental landscape features.

The business serves homeowners, landscape designers, and art collectors looking to enhance patio spaces or gardens with resilient, weather-tolerant artwork. By offering a curated selection of metalwork, it contributes to the aesthetic variety of residential and commercial properties in the Grand Valley region.

Visitors to the showroom can expect to find an extensive inventory that emphasizes Southwestern themes, wildlife representations, and whimsical novelty items. The venue operates as a retail destination where customers can view the scale and craftsmanship of the pieces firsthand.

Notable Features & Attributes

Wildlife Collection

The inventory features life-sized and scaled representations of diverse fauna, including horses, bears, donkeys, rams, bulls, eagles, vultures, giraffes, dinosaurs, and bigfoot.

Material Craftsmanship

Many pieces are hand-crafted and incorporate mixed metals, utilizing brass and copper enhancements to add color variation and detail to the steel structures.

Botanical Sculptures

Beyond animal figures, the shop offers permanent botanical decor, including colorful metal flowers and agave plants designed for xeriscaping accents.

Whimsical Novelty Items

The selection includes humorous and character-based works, such as the distinct "chile guys" playing bongo drums, catering to eclectic tastes.
