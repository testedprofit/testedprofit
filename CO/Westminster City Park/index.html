205 Acres of Community

Westminster City Park

More than just fields & grass. A regional hub for recreation, reflection, and hidden history.

Plan Your Visit Read the Secrets

205 Acres

Regional park scale featuring soccer fields, softball complexes, and running trails.

Dog Friendly

Open 24 hours. Includes playgrounds, pavilions for gatherings, and pet access.

Tribute Garden

Home to a stunning tribute garden with over 1,600 engraved pavers and sculptures.

The Deep Dive

Details most visitors never learn about.

History

The "Ghost" Memorial

Planned site for a Lao Hmong Memorial featuring a T-28 Trojan Warbird. In a strange twist, the project was relocated in 2025, and the specific plane intended for display crashed in California.

Conservation

Hidden Habitats

Sustainability goes deep here. Trees removed during renovations weren't discarded; they were repurposed to create underwater fish habitats in nearby water bodies.

Sports

Disc Golf Resurrection

Closed in 2023, redesigned and reopened in late 2025. The current 9-hole layout is just "Phase 1" of a larger plan to restore the full 18-hole experience.

Visitor Essentials

Timing the Water

Fountain operates Memorial Day - Labor Day. Winter offers a stark, quiet reflective experience.

Parking Strategy

Lots near Christopher Fields fill fast. Use the smaller east side lots for the Tribute Garden.

Hours

General park: Sunrise to Sunset.
Tribute Garden: Open 24 hours.
