The Hayden Range

Routt County Public Firing Range

No Fees
3 Miles S of Hwy 40

Facility Details

Managed by CPW, this facility is a community staple for sighting in rifles and recreational shooting. It is strictly a Pack-In, Pack-Out facility.

  • Operating Hours Daily, Sunrise to Sunset.
  • Amenities Concrete benches, berms (Rifle/Pistol).
  • Distinction This is the Public range. The nearby "Club" is private/keycard only.

Stewardship

  • Paper Targets Only
  • Clean Up Your Brass
  • No Glass / No Explosives
