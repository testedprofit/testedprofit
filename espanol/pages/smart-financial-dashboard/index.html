What is a Smart Financial Dashboard?

A smart financial dashboard is more than just a spreadsheet. It is an automated tool that aggregates data to provide actionable insights into your net worth, asset allocation, and projected growth. Unlike static trackers, a smart dashboard uses variables like inflation, compound interest frequency, and dynamic contributions to give you a realistic picture of your financial future.
