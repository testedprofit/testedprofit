The Visionary Artist

Gary Lee Price

Renowned American sculptor Gary Lee Price created this masterpiece as part of his "Great Contributors Series," aiming to immortalize figures who have profoundly shaped our history.

Humanizing Genius

Interactive Art

Price's goal was to strip away the intimidating aura of "genius" and present a relatable, human Einstein. The sculpture is a companion, not just a monument, inviting warm interaction.
