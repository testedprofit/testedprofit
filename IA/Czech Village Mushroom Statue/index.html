Creation Story

Carved by chainsaw artist Nate Greiner from a tree damaged during the 2020 Derecho, giving the art a "silver lining" history.

Key Features

"Houby" Heritage

"Houby" is Czech for mushrooms. The statue celebrates the region's strong Czech culture and mushroom hunting traditions.

Derecho Survivor

Created from a tree stump damaged in the severe August 2020 windstorm, symbolizing resilience.

Houby Days

Commemorates the annual May festival when the village bustles with music and fresh morel mushroom vendors.
