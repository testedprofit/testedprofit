About the Art

Created by Cliff Mann as a humorous tribute to Iowa's agricultural identity. Often compared to Dublin, Ohio's "Field of Corn."

Key Features

Whimsical Design

Mimics the layout of the prehistoric Stonehenge monument using massive concrete corn cobs.

Detailed Craftsmanship

Each cob is several feet tall and features detailed, textured kernels to capture the essence of Iowa's famous crop.

Lowe Park Amenities

Part of a larger art collection that includes the Klopfenstein Amphitheater, walking trails, and discovery gardens.
