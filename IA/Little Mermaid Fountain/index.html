Danish Heritage

Honors Hans Christian Andersen. Purchased by "Progressive Danes of Kimballton" in 1974, recast in bronze in 2008.

Key Features

The Sculpture

An exact replica of the Copenhagen original. Originally fiberglass, it was recast in bronze for year-round display.

Sculpture Garden

Features 8 other bronze statues of Andersen's characters, like "The Ugly Duckling" and "The Emperor's New Clothes".

Audio Tour

Visitors can call a phone number at each sculpture to listen to a synopsis of the relevant fairy tale.

Nearby Attractions

Danish Windmill (Elk Horn)

Just three miles away in Elk Horn, visitors can see an authentic Danish Windmill, transported from Denmark and reassembled in Iowa.
