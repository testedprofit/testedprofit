Who was Harold Keller?

Harold "Pie" Keller (1921–1979) was a Corporal in the 5th Marine Division and a Purple Heart recipient who fought at Midway, Guadalcanal, and Bougainville before Iwo Jima.

Despite his historic role, he was known for extreme humility and rarely spoke of his service; his own children did not know he was in the famous photograph until decades after his death. After the war, he returned to Brooklyn and served as the city's fire chief for 30 years.
