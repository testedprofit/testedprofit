Altoona, IA

Moai
Dude

An 11-foot tall, 1,800-pound rusted steel sculpture with a "surfer-dude" smirk.

Location

Ironwood Park
2222 3rd Ave SW, Altoona, IA 50009

Wheelchair Accessible

Significance

Unveiled in 2013 by artist John Brommel. This was Altoona's first public sculpture, created from recycled steel.

Key Features

The Smirk

Features a laid-back "surfer-dude" smirk, unlike traditional stoic Moai.

Recycled Steel

Made from recycled, rusted steel, giving it a distinct, textured charm.

Photo Op

A playful, modern reimagining that serves as a fun backdrop for visitors.
