Why Crowdfund

Built for trust.

Goal Enforcement

Smart contract holds all funds. Your goal must be met or every donor receives an automatic refund — no exceptions, no discretion.

Zero Platform Cut

No percentage taken. You pay only the Algorand network fee — roughly 0.002 ALGO per transaction. Every ALGO raised goes to the creator.

Invite-Only Listings

Every campaign is manually vetted before going live. No rugs, no spam projects — only legitimate builders with real plans.

Process

How it works

1

Apply via Twitter

Reach out to @ProfitnetPNET on Twitter with your project details. The team reviews every application before approval.

2

Campaign Listed On-Chain

Once approved, your campaign is deployed as an Algorand smart contract. Goal, deadline, and creator address are all immutable on-chain.

3

Funds Released or Refunded

Donors fund the campaign directly. If the goal is met, the contract releases funds to the creator. If it falls short, every donor is automatically refunded — no action required.
