Features

Built for trust, enforced by code

Every rule is written into the smart contract. No admin can override it.

On-Chain Escrow

All contributions are locked in the smart contract until the campaign goal is met. No one — not even the creator — can access the funds early.

Fully Transparent

Every contribution, the running total, contributor count, and percentage to goal are visible on-chain in real time. Anyone can audit the campaign.

Automatic Refunds

If the goal isn't met by the deadline, the smart contract enforces refunds to every contributor automatically. Zero risk of creator exit with the funds.

Process

How it works

1

Connect your wallet

Open AlgoFlow and connect your Pera Wallet. Switch to TestNet to try for free — no real ALGO required.

2

Create your campaign

Enter a campaign name, description, ALGO goal amount, and deadline. The smart contract is deployed in one transaction.

3

Share your campaign link

Share the campaign with your community. Anyone with an Algorand wallet can contribute — all contributions visible on-chain in real time.

4

Withdraw when funded

When the goal is met, withdraw all funds to your wallet. If the deadline passes without hitting the goal, contributors are automatically refunded.
