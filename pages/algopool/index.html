Features

The fairest lottery on Algorand

No house advantage beyond the 10% split. Every entry verified on-chain. Every draw verifiable by anyone.

90% Winner Payout

The highest winner payout of any on-chain lottery — 90% of all entries go directly to a single winner. The split is enforced by smart contract and cannot be changed.

Verifiable Randomness

Winner is selected by block_hash mod total_entries. Anyone can verify the draw using public Algorand block data — no oracle or off-chain RNG to trust.

Transparent Entry List

All participant addresses and entry transactions are publicly visible on Algorand. Anyone can audit the full entry list before and after the draw — no hidden participants.

Process

How it works

1

Connect your wallet

Open AlgoFlow and connect via Pera Wallet. Use TestNet to enter a practice round without risking real ALGO.

2

Click Enter (1 ALGO)

Sign a 1 ALGO transaction to enter the current week's pool. Your entry is immediately visible on-chain alongside all other participants.

3

Wait for the weekly draw

At the end of the week, the draw block is reached. The smart contract uses that block's hash modulo the total entry count to select the winner index.

4

Winner announced and paid automatically

The winning address receives 90% of the total pool directly from the smart contract. No claim button — the payout is automatic and immediate.
