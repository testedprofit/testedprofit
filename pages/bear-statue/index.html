The Genesis of Barnaby

At 12600 W Colfax Ave, a magnificent wooden bear carved from a colossal ponderosa pine stands as a local icon. Known as "Barnaby," he was born from a lightning-struck tree that refused to fall.

The founders of TestedProfit discovered this site during a discovery hike—a tradition used to scout untapped potential. They saw in this timber what they see in every project: the inherent strength waiting to be revealed through precise, purposeful action.

Carved by the master artisan Elias, Barnaby represents the bridge between raw nature and refined strategy. Every cut of the chisel echoes the TestedProfit methodology—revealing the masterpiece within the rugged exterior.
