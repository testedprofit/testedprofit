The Cowboy in Pajamas

A 20-foot whimsical twist on Western tradition in the heart of Denver.

About the Piece

Standing 20 feet tall, this bronze sculpture features a cowboy dressed in colorful pajamas, offering a playful and unexpected twist on traditional Western imagery. It challenges the rugged mythology of the American West with a sense of humor and domestic vulnerability.

Artist: Sean O'Meallie
Established: 2008

Community Voice

"Art either speaks to you, or it doesn't. To me, this sculpture is a total delight. It kinda reminds me of Yosemite Sam, but from a Toy Story perspective. Could also be kind of a commentary on the mythology embodied in the Hollywood version of the history of the American West."

— Local Visitor Review
