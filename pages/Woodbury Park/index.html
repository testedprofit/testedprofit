A Safe & Welcoming Place for All

To ensure a positive experience for everyone, please respect the following rules.

No Alcohol

Alcoholic beverages are not permitted within the park.

Smoke-Free Zone

Smoking of any kind, including marijuana, is strictly prohibited.

Respectful Conduct

Public lewdness or inappropriate behavior will not be tolerated.

Always Improving For You

Through the "Woodbury Park Improvements Project," we've invested significantly to enhance your park experience. This dedication to growth ensures Woodbury remains a premier destination for our community.

Enhanced Accessibility: New pathways and surfaces for all visitors.

Six-Acre Expansion: More room for future amenities and green space.

Future Dugout Construction: Continuing to improve our sports facilities.
