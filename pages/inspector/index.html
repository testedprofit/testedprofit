Risk Rating System

CLEAN

No clawback-enabled tokens. All held assets are safe.

LOW RISK

A small number of clawback-enabled ASAs detected. Proceed with awareness.

CAUTION

Multiple clawback-enabled tokens held. Significant risk — tokens can be seized by issuers.

Clawback-enabled tokens (marked RISKY) can be removed from your wallet by the token creator without your consent.

Features

Know what you're holding

Before you trade, receive, or hold an asset — inspect the wallet. Knowledge is protection.

Risk Score

Instantly see which held tokens have clawback authority enabled — flagged as RISKY. The overall wallet rating (Clean / Low Risk / Caution) is calculated from risky asset count.

View all ALGO, fungible tokens, and NFTs held by the address. Fungible tokens and NFTs are separated and clearly labeled for easy review.

No Connection Required
