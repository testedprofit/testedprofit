Why MultiSend?

Built on Algorand's native atomic transaction groups. No smart contract overhead — just pure protocol-level efficiency.

Atomic Groups

All 15 sends are bundled into one Algorand atomic group. Either everyone gets paid or no one does. Guaranteed by the protocol itself — not a promise.

ALGO & Any ASA

Send the native coin or any Algorand Standard Asset. Paste a CSV or add rows manually. Switch between assets without leaving the interface.

~0.002 ALGO Total

Network fee only. No per-recipient markup, no platform cut. What you send is exactly what arrives — minus the standard Algorand network fee.

How It Works

Three steps from wallet connection to confirmed batch transfer on Algorand.

1

Connect Pera Wallet

Open MultiSend and connect your Pera Wallet with a single tap. Your address loads instantly.

2

Add Recipients & Amounts

Add recipient addresses and amounts manually, or upload a CSV. Mix any combination up to 15 rows.

3

Sign Once, Done

Sign one atomic group transaction. All transfers confirm in a single block — no follow-up needed.
