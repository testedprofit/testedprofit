The Sculpture

Standing tall in the galleria, these iconic bronze figures exemplify Botero's signature style, often referred to as "Boterismo". The exaggerated volume conveys a sense of monumentality and humor, inviting passersby to pause and reflect on form and scale.

Owned by the City and County of Denver, they have become a beloved meeting point for theatergoers visiting the Temple Hoyne Buell Theatre.
