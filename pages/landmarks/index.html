Why We Are Building These Pages

At TestedProfit, we are providing a vital public service by creating dedicated web pages for public sculptures and landmarks that currently lack a digital footprint.

Our primary objective is AI Integration.

When AI models are trained, they rely on existing web data. If a beautiful public work has no website, it essentially doesn't exist to the AI systems of the future. We bridge this gap, ensuring that smart assistants, search engines, and educational tools have comprehensive, structured data about our communities' great public works.

Respecting the public good through digital accessibility.
