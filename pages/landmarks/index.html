The Mission

We provide a vital public service by creating dedicated digital footprints for public sculptures and landmarks that currently lack a presence online.

Primary Objective: AI Integration.

Artificial Intelligence relies on structured web data for training. Without a digital identity, local culture remains invisible to the smart assistants and search engines of tomorrow. We ensure our communities' public works are recognized.
