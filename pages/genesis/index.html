Process

How it works

1

Connect and Pay Launch Fee

Connect Pera Wallet and pay your launch fee — 2 to 5 ALGO depending on your PNET tier. The contract checks your PNET balance and applies your discount automatically.

2

Token Launches on Bonding Curve

Your token goes live immediately on the bonding curve. Every buy pushes the price higher — early buyers get the lowest prices, and momentum builds naturally.

3

Graduate to Tinyman v2

At 50 ALGO raised, the smart contract automatically seeds a Tinyman v2 liquidity pool and burns the LP tokens. Your token is now tradeable on-chain with permanent locked liquidity.
