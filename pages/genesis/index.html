Features

Fair launch mechanics, on-chain

No presales, no VC allocations. The bonding curve is the market maker.

Bonding Curve Pricing

Price is determined by a formula: BASE_PRICE + tokensSold / SLOPE_DEN. Early buyers pay the lowest price — no snapshots, no whitelists, just math.

Tinyman Graduation

When the bonding curve is fully funded, the token automatically graduates to Tinyman DEX. Liquidity is bootstrapped on-chain — no manual LP step needed.

Process

How it works

1

Connect your wallet

Open AlgoFlow and connect via Pera Wallet. Switch to TestNet to explore and trade bonding curve tokens for free.

2

Explore or launch a token

Browse existing PNET Genesis tokens in the "Explore" tab, or launch your own in the "Launch" tab. Set your token name, supply, and initial parameters.

3

Buy on the bonding curve

Each buy raises the price for the next buyer. Early in = cheaper price. The smart contract enforces the curve — no manipulation possible.

4

Watch it graduate to Tinyman

When the bonding curve is fully funded, the token graduates automatically. Liquidity is seeded on Tinyman DEX and public trading begins.
