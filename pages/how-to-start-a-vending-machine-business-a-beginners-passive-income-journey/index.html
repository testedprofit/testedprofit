Choosing Machine & Tech

Hardware Strategy

  • Snack vs. Drink vs. Combo:
    Combo machines are excellent for starters. Dedicated machines work best in high-volume locations.
  • New vs. Refurbished:
    New ($3k-$7k): Warranty included, lower risk.
    Refurbished ($1.5k-$3.5k): Great value, but buy from reputable dealers.

Essential Tech

  • Cashless Readers:
    Non-negotiable. Readers (e.g., Nayax, Cantaloupe) can boost sales by 20-50% in the modern cashless economy.
  • Inventory Management (VMS):
    Software that tells you exactly what sold. Prevents wasted trips and optimizes stock levels.
