Features

Social media — without the platform

No account. No moderation. No deplatforming. Your words, on-chain, forever.

Immutable Posts

Every post is stored in an Algorand transaction note. Transaction notes are immutable — they can never be edited, deleted, or censored by anyone, including the poster.

Hashtag Filtering

Use #hashtags in your posts to make them discoverable. The feed supports hashtag filtering — find all posts on any topic instantly across the full on-chain history.

Reply & Quote

Reply to any post or quote a post with your own commentary. Full conversation threading — all stored on-chain. No centralized server to go down or get hacked.

Process

How it works

1

Connect your wallet

Open AlgoSocial and connect via Pera Wallet. Your Algorand address is your identity — no username or email needed.

2

Write your post

Type your message. Add #hashtags for discoverability. Include URLs. Reply to or quote any existing on-chain post.

3

Sign a 0-ALGO transaction

Your post is submitted as the note field of a 0-ALGO Algorand transaction. You pay only the network fee (~0.001 ALGO). Pera Wallet signs it in seconds.

4

Post lives on-chain forever

Your message is now part of the Algorand blockchain — immutable, public, and permanent. It appears in the feed for all AlgoSocial users instantly.
