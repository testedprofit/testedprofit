How It Works

Three steps from thought to immutable on-chain record — no servers in the middle.

1

Connect Pera Wallet

Open AlgoSocial and connect your Pera Wallet. Your address becomes your identity — no registration, no username creation.

2

Write Your Post

Compose your message (up to 900 bytes) with optional hashtags and reply references. The content is encoded directly into a transaction note field.

3

Sign & Publish Forever

Sign the transaction — your post is confirmed in seconds and permanently recorded on the Algorand blockchain. No takedowns possible.
