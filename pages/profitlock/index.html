Non-Custodial

The smart contract holds your funds. Not us, not a multisig — only the depositing wallet can ever unlock them. We have no access, no keys, no override capability.

Blockchain-Enforced Maturity

ALGO & Any ASA

How It Works

Three steps to lock your assets and enforce your own holding discipline on-chain.

1

Connect & Choose Lock Period

Connect your Pera Wallet and select your lock period — 30, 90, 180, or 365 days. The block round is calculated automatically.

2

Enter Amount to Lock

Choose ALGO or any ASA and enter the amount you want to lock in the vault. Review the maturity block round before signing.

3

Sign, Wait, Claim

Sign the deposit transaction. The contract holds your funds until the block round is reached — then you return to claim with one more signature.
