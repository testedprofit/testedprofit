Process

How it works

1

Connect your wallet

Open AlgoFlow ProfitLock and connect via Pera Wallet. Use TestNet to test the lock flow without risking real assets.

2

Choose lock duration

Select 30, 90, or 180 days — or 1 year. This sets the exact unlock timestamp written immutably into the smart contract.

3

Enter amount and sign

Enter the amount of ALGO or ASA to lock, then sign the transaction in Pera Wallet. Your assets are immediately transferred to the vault smart contract.

4

Funds release automatically at unlock time

When the countdown hits zero, the smart contract releases the funds back to your wallet automatically. No action needed — the contract enforces it.
