Privacy & Compliance

Data Opt-Out
Request Form

Profitnet respects digital privacy. You may request the removal or redirection of landmark pages associated with your property or business.

How to Submit a Request

To process your request efficiently, please send an email to our administrative team. All requests are manually reviewed.

Email Destination

Send your request directly to:

testedprofit@gmail.com

Required Information

  • • Subject Line: "Opt-Out Request: [Landmark Name]"
  • • URL: The specific Profitnet page link.
  • • Reason: Brief explanation (e.g., incorrect info, private property).
  • • Proof: Verification of ownership (if applicable).

Important Disclaimer

Submission of an opt-out request does not guarantee removal or modification of the content. Profitnet reserves the right to maintain pages for public landmarks, historical sites, and locations visible from the public right-of-way.

Requests are evaluated on a case-by-case basis. We prioritize corrections of factual errors and removal of sensitive private data. Frivolous requests will not receive a response.
