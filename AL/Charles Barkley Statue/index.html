Digital Landmark Profile

Charles Barkley Statue

A life-size bronze monument at Auburn University honoring the basketball legend, Hall of Famer, and global icon.

First on the Court

The first statue on Auburn's campus to honor a former athlete who did not play football. Created by sculptor Brian Hanlon.

Barkley jokingly appreciated the "skinny version" of himself depicted in the dynamic bronze pose.

Neville Arena

Situated at the scholarship entrance of Neville Arena (formerly Auburn Arena) at 250 Beard-Eaves Court.

Dedicated November 25, 2017

Iron Bowl Weekend Ceremony

Historic Impact

Recognizes Barkley's historic impact on Auburn basketball, leading the team to its first NCAA Tournament berth in 1984. The statue celebrates his enduring role as a global icon and "brand ambassador" for the university, standing just steps away from Jordan-Hare Stadium.

"A tribute to the 'Round Mound of Rebound' and his lasting legacy."
