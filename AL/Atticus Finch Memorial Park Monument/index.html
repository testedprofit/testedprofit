Highest Calling

Dedicated in 1997 by the Alabama State Bar to honor Atticus Finch, the fictional lawyer from To Kill a Mockingbird.

The plaque cites Finch as a model for the "legal profession's highest calling" and a "symbol of the best in the American lawyer."

Literary Capital

Located on the grounds of the Old Courthouse Museum in downtown Monroeville.

Hometown of Harper Lee

Inspiration for the novel's setting

Integrity. Courage. Justice.

The large granite block stands as a permanent reminder that fictional heroes can shape real-world values. The nearby Old Courthouse served as the model for the courtroom scenes in the novel and the 1962 film, cementing Monroeville's place in American literary history.
