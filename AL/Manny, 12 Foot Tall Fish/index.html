Digital Landmark Profile

Manny the Bass

A 12-foot jumping largemouth bass statue in downtown Eufaula, Alabama. A proud tribute to the "Bass Capital of the World."

A Tale of Two Mannys

Inspired by Tom Mann, founder of Mann's Bait Company. The original statue stood at Tom Mann's Fish World.

The current statue is a 2010 fiberglass replica, commissioned by the city to replace the original that fell into disrepair.

Main Street Catch

Located at the intersection of East Broad St. & Randolph Ave.

Jumping from a planter box in the street

Bass Capital of the World

Manny isn't just a statue; he's a symbol of Eufaula's deep connection to the shores of Lake Eufaula. He celebrates the economic and cultural impact of the fishing industry, paying homage to the days when Mann's Bait Company was the city's largest employer.

"A fiberglass tribute to the legendary catches of Southeast Alabama."
