Digital Landmark Profile

Bo Jackson Statue

A bronze monument at Auburn University honoring one of the most versatile athletes in sports history.

Larger Than Life

A 1.5x life-size bronze sculpture weighing 1,900 pounds, depicting Jackson in a dynamic running pose.

Honors his 1985 Heisman Trophy season (1,786 rushing yards).

Heisman Plaza

Located on the east side of Jordan-Hare Stadium (470-598 Heisman Dr).

Dedicated April 14, 2012

Part of the Heisman Winner Trio

Legacy & Tradition

Created by Montana sculptor Ken Bjorge, the statue stands alongside those of Pat Sullivan and Cam Newton. It serves as a popular gathering spot for students and fans, especially during gameday "Tiger Walks." Nearby, a bust of legendary coach John Heisman connects the past to the present.

"Celebrating the 40th anniversary of a legendary win."
