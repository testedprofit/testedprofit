Digital Landmark Profile

Angel of Hope

A memorial statue in Westgate Park dedicated to all parents who have lost a child. A sanctuary for comfort, healing, and remembrance.

Literary Inspiration

Inspired by the bestselling book The Christmas Box by Richard Paul Evans, where a mother mourns her child at an angel's base.

The word "HOPE" is engraved on the statue's right wing, offering a message of resilience to grieving families.

Wings of Peace

The statue depicts an angel with outstretched arms and an upward gaze, symbolizing hope. For many parents without a physical gravesite to visit, this memorial serves as a vital touchstone for reflection and connection.

"A special place to visit and reflect, regardless of the child's age."
