The Big Peanut

A prominent, large, and quirky fiberglass statue in Dothan, Alabama—the heart of the "Peanut Capital of the World."

Golden Glory

A giant fiberglass peanut, painted realistically brown and yellow.

Location: 5622 S Oates St, Dothan, AL

Setting: Entrance to the National Peanut Festival fairgrounds.

Capital of the World

Highlights the immense importance of the peanut crop to the Dothan and Houston County economy.

The area is responsible for a large percentage of the entire U.S. peanut crop, making this statue a symbol of regional pride.

National Peanut Festival

Dothan hosts the world's largest peanut festival, an annual tradition since 1991. The "Big Peanut" stands guard at the festival entrance year-round, serving as a beloved photo-op for locals and tourists alike.

"A quirky tribute to the legume that powers the local economy."
