The Bronze Bomber

A life-size bronze monument in Tuscaloosa honoring Deontay Wilder, the native son and former WBC heavyweight champion.

Capturing the Champ

Sculpted by Tuscaloosa artist Caleb O’Connor.

  • 7 feet tall, 830 pounds
  • Literal tribute to "The Bronze Bomber" nickname

Visit the Legend

Located at the Tuscaloosa Tourism & Sports building (River Market).

1900 Jack Warner Parkway

Open 24/7 along the Riverwalk

Beacon of Hope

Unveiled on May 25, 2022, this statue stands as a symbol of black excellence for future generations. Wilder, the first American heavyweight champion in nearly a decade, personally noted the importance of having his likeness immortalized in a city with a complex racial history.

"A hometown tribute to a champion, captured in a boxing stance with a chiseled torso and sinewy arms."
