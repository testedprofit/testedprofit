Strategic Context

Oakhurst Park II operates within Westminster's Resource Management Program (Tier Service Level Program), established in 2011. This program uses tiered maintenance cycles to ensure professional-grade park quality while optimizing municipal resources.

The park fulfills the "Half-Mile Access Goal," ensuring critical green infrastructure is distributed geographically within 0.5 miles of all residential units.
